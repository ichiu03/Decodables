Max, a boy who loves games and Lego, finds math tough. His teacher organized a math competition with a new Lego set as the prize. Max decides to participate in the competition, despite knowing the puzzles will be tough. Determined to get better at math, Max practices every day and helps when needed. Through hard work and practice, Max learns that practicing is the key to success. As the competition day nears, Max feels a mix of excitement and readiness. Despite his nerves, Max competes well and wins. Max understands that hard work can help him overcome challenges. Math becomes enjoyable as Max watches the rewards of his hard work. Max makes a promise to keep practicing math and help his friends get better too.
