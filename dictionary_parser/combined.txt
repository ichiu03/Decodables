One sunny day, Tom ran in the park with his playful dog, Max, enjoying the lush green fields and towering trees. Amid their play, Tom noticed a tiny, scared puppy. "Hello, little one. Are you lost?" Tom asked in a gentle tone, as the pup, named Sparky, whimpered softly. Concerned, Tom decided to aid Sparky in finding his family, guiding him through challenges like crossing a busy street. With persistence and kindness, Tom persevered, embarking on an venture to reunite Sparky with his loved ones.
