Mike loved dogs, and in his neighborhood, his family had a happy dog friend. On a sunny weekend, Mike decided to explore Dog Grove, where he met Max, a fast greyhound, and Moon, a smart herding dog. One sad mutt in Dog Grove caught his interest The mutt shared a story from its past, inspiring Mike to find it a caring home. Instead of giving up on the dog at the Grove, Mike and his family kept trying. They chose the special dog In the end, Mike realized that every dog deserves love.
