Chapter 1: Tom and the Lost Pup On a sunny day, Tom ran through the park with Max, his playful dog. Tom enjoyed the park's lush green fields and towering trees. As they frolicked about, Tom spotted a tiny, Scared puppy. "Hello, little one. Are you lost?" Tom asked in a soothing tone. The pup, named Sparky, let out a soft cry. concerned for Sparky, Tom made up his mind to help the pup find his family. Guiding Sparky through the park, they encountered challenges such as crossing a busy street. Despite the obstacles, Tom remained determined and compassionate. Together, they set off on an expedition to reunite Sparky with his family.
