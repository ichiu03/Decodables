section 1: The Bark Brigade Saves the Day In a cozy district brimming with woofing joy, there resided a team of dogs with unique traits. There was Luna, the fast runner, Max, the good leaper, and Rosie, the great sniffer. Together, they formed a team named the Bark Brigade, always ready to give a hand to those in need. One sunny morning, a kitten named Whiskers went missing. Without hesitation, the Bark Brigade leaped into action. Luna raced ahead, Max sprang soar to look around, and Rosie sniffed out a route. next their abilities, they found Whiskers lodged up in a giant tree, meowing for aid.
