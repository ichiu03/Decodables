section 1: peter and the Magic Math Tree One sunny afternoon, peter ventured into the woods within his house. Amongst the giant trees and singing owls, he faltered find a strange view. A tree different any other, shining with digits and signs dancing around its trunk. Mesmerized, peter warily crept. To his amazement, the tree communicated, "I am the Magic Math Tree, here to assist you with your math adventures." Excitement sparkled inside peter as he eagerly received the tree's offer. With each math problem cracked, peter's confidence grew. As the sun started to set, peter exited the tree, brimming with newfound excitement for math.
