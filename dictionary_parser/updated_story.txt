Chapter 1: Max's Test Day Adventure Max, a curious 10-year-old boy, loved learning new things. Today, he was excited about the science test. Arriving at school with his friends, Max felt a little nervous. When Max got his test, he started answering the questions with confidence. But soon, Max faced a tough question he didn't know how to answer. Max remembered his teacher's advice to try his best and think through problems. He closed his eyes and thought about his favorite science experiment. Remembering his teacher's advice and using what he knew, Max felt proud after solving the question and finishing the test.
