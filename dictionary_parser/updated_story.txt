Once upon a time, there was a boy named John, who was ten years old and loved discovering new things. After class, he hurried home, excited for his lunchtime tradition. On a bright morning, he sat at his desk with his biology manual and notes, ready to learn about photosynthesis. Enthralled by how plants form their nourishment, he quickly devoured his sandwich but soon felt a stomachache as a result. Realizing his mistake, John took a deep breath and decided to enjoy his meal without rushing. Enjoying each bite, he savored the delicious smells and felt his discomfort fade. He tidied up and went for a walk outdoors, thinking about balancing work and play. Taking his time during meals, John promised to always unwind. With a new perspective, John happily returned home, eager to keep exploring. The End
