Chapter 1: Manny was an creative, curious 8-year-old boy who loved to explore the Realm around him Manny's family got a new device, and he loved it right away. The bright screen and endless possibilities captivated him He played games and watched videos on the device, getting lost in its digital world. As days passed, Manny noticed strange behavior from the device. It would randomly rotate in the middle of the night, play music during his classes at school, and even send silly messages to his friends without his understanding. Manny's friends and family started to worry. He was always glued to the screen, neglecting everything around him. They tried to encourage him to put the device down and spend time with them instead. But Manny was too fascinated by the device. He neglected his assignments and chores, causing his grades to slip and his room to become a mess His bonds suffered as he barely spoke to his friends and family, lost in the digital Realm within the device One day, Manny realized he had become disconnected from the real Realm while alone with his device. The screen no longer brought him joy. He felt lonely and regretful for ignoring those who cared about him. Manny's parents decided to intervene. They limited his device use and encouraged him to play outside, read books, and spend time with loved ones. Manny learned to balance his technology use and show restraint. He said sorry to his friends and family for not spending time with them and tried to spend more time with them in person Manny rediscovered the simple joys of life without his device, realizing a valuable lesson. There was more to life than screens and technology Manny found true happiness in the Realm around him, grateful for his encounters. The story of Manny and the Mischievous device ends with a lesson learned about maintaining a healthy bond with technology.
